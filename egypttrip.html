Up and Down the Nile

During our time in Egypt we traveled through many different cities:
  • Alexandria
  • Cairo
  • Aswan
  • Edfu
  • Luxor
Then back to Cairo.

We flew from Cairo to Aswan and started on a cruise down the Nile. Along the cruise we saw multiple temples and cities. The temple of Horus is in the picture below, and was the largest temple front that we saw. The largest known temple is Karnak in Luxor.

Luxor was the last stop on the cruise and there we visited the Valley of the Kings and got to ride in a hot air balloon during the sunrise. The picture below is in King Tuts tomb and below that are pictures from the hot air balloon ride.
